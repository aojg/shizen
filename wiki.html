The game contains a myriad of what I call crop properties; these are
essentially just factors that can affect a crop in some manner. Examples
of these properties include things like temperature, humidity, and sunlight exposure.

two crops which we'll call A and B. Crop A may favour sunlight very highly, i.e. this
